  AIMessage("Nice to meet you Aayush"),
  HumanMessage("What is my name?")
]
Why does the bot remember?

Every time you send a new message, the entire chat_history is sent along with your latest message to Gemini.

Gemini reads:
  • "My name is Aayush"
  • "Nice to meet you Aayush"
  • "What is my name?"
Since the earlier message contains your name, Gemini can answer: "Your name is Aayush."
